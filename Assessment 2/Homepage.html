Covid-19 Precautions

Unfortunately Covid‐19 cases are still on the rise. As the safety and well being of students is our main priority, most of our events and challenges will be taking place online on zoom.
However, some balloon keepy uppy events are permitted to take place in person along with social events provided we follow the necessary Covid‐19 precautions.

For our Club events and gatherings the following Covid‐19 safety measures apply:

  • Social distancing of 2 meters
  • Wearing of face masks in crowded areas outdoors and movement indoors
  • Maximum of 6 people at a table for social events
  • Regular hand washing
  • EU Covid certs may be needed for some locations
For more information visit https://www2.hse.ie/conditions

About us

Welcome to the MTU balloon keepy uppy club! This club was set up for the youth around Cork who are stuck at home during the Covid-19 lockdowns.
It is designed to be a fun and exciting way for people to exercise at home. We encourage you to take part in the fun and challenge yourself by taking part in our online events.

What is balloon keepy upppy?

This is a recent sport that gained popularity during Covid-19. Players throw a balloon up in the air and must try to keep it off the gorund.
