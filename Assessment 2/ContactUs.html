FAQs

Are the events online or in person?

Due to current restrictions with Covid-19, we will try to have as many events scheduled as we can on zoom, but some events will be held in person with necessary precautions.

What is Balloon Keepy Uppy?

Balloon Keepy Uppy is a sport where players must keep the balloon off the ground. See our Homepage for more information.

Where are the trainings held?

Training is generally held in the Student Center at MTU, you can see more details on scheduled events and locations on our Events page
